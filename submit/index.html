Submit

The DollyCustom submit page allows you to send your information to be listed on this site. After your info is received, it will be checked to enter the list.

You just need to:

  • Have created and sold at least 10 custom Blythe dolls
  • Fill the following form
  • Click submit
  • Wait until you see the confirmation page. If the form isn't working, attach the photos to an email and send them to dollycustom@missblythe.com

All submissions are checked and you will receive an email saying if any other information is needed or if all is okay. New entries are added on a monthly basis, on the first Saturday of each month. You should receive a notification as soon as I process it.

Note: Most submissions that meet the conditions are accepted, but I reserve the right of not listing a customizer that doesn't have the quality of work present in the current list.

  • About You:

  • Your Custom Work:

  •   Check box if you accept requests for custom work

  • Your Images:

  • Each customizer page has a place for a large image (landscape/horizontal) and other images for the gallery.

    Make sure your images have max. 2000px size (in their largest dimension).

    (If you can't send the images using this form, please send them to: dollycustom@missblythe.com)

  • Your Social Links:

  Check box to you confirm that you have created and sold more than 10 Custom Blythe dolls.

Make sure that when submitting this form, you see the confirmation page. If the form doesn't go through, please resubmit again. If you are sending large photos, you need to wait a little as the images are being sent.
