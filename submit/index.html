Submit

The DollyCustom submit page allows you to send your information to be listed on this site.

You just need to:

  • Have created and sold at least 10 custom Blythe dolls
  • Fill the following form
  • Add a photo of one of your custom dolls (horizontal / landscape orientation)
  • Click submit
  • Wait until you see the confirmation page


After your info is received, it will be checked to enter the list.

Note: Most submissions that meet the condition are accepted, but I reserve the right of not listing a customizer that doesn’t have the quality of work present in the current list. Make sure that when submitting this form, you see the confirmation page. If the form doesn’t go through, please try and resubmit again.
