Submit

The DollyCustom submit page allows you to send your information to be featured in this site.

You just need to:

  • Have created and sold more than 10 custom Blythe dolls
  • Fill the following form
  • Add a photo of one of your custom dolls (horizontal / landscape orientation)
  • Click submit


After your info is received, it will be confirmed and enter the list.


Fill the form

  • Check box if you accept commissions.

  • Check box to you confirm that you have created/sold more than 10 Blythe Custom dolls.
